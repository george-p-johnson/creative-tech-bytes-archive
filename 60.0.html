Runway Research | Introducing Act-One

Runway has introduced Act-One, a new tool designed to generate expressive character animations from simple video inputs, released within their Gen-3 Alpha platform. Act-One allows creators to transform an actor‚Äôs performance, captured with minimal equipment, into digital characters, preserving intricate details like eye-lines, micro-expressions, and pacing. Anthropic publicly releases AI tool that can take over the user‚Äôs mouse cursor

Anthropic has publicly released "Computer Use," a new feature within its Claude 3.5 model that allows the AI to control a user's mouse cursor to perform tasks on their computer. Available via API with the mid-range Sonnet model, this tool enables multi-step actions based on visual cues from screenshots, allowing Claude to "see" the screen, move the cursor, click buttons, and type text. #Sports

MLS launching NFT-based game platform with Sweet

NFT platform Sweet has announced MLS Quest, a blockchain-based collectibles marketplace and gaming platform developed in partnership with Major League Soccer (MLS) and the MLS Players Association. MLS Quest allows fans to collect NFTs in the form of virtual medallions featuring highlights from MLS matches, which can be redeemed for real-world rewards or traded with others. This initiative marks Sweet's second major sports league collaboration after launching the NHL Breakaway platform. Unlike previous NFT initiatives, which focused heavily on monetization, MLS Quest emphasizes gameplay and fan engagement, allowing users to start for free by collecting medallions via QR codes available at games, on broadcasts, and online. Revenue streams for MLS Quest will eventually include medallion pack sales, peer-to-peer royalties, and a premium subscription, although initial focus is on user experience rather than direct sales.
